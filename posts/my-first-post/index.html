The Great Orchestra of Christmas Charity

October 19, 2023

The Great Orchestra of Christmas Charity is a very well known charity in Poland. It helps hospitals and patients by funding medical equipment. However they don’t do it like most other charities by asking for money on every ocasion. They make helping sick people accually fun. Once a year, they organize what they call a “GRAND FINALE”. During it they organise Concerts and other event’s. They make soup and flatbread for everyone that donates. They show of vintage cars, they dance, they let you print out hearts on a 3D printer, they bring dogs, retired solidiers tell wartime stories, you can play VR, or get into a basket lift, even the fire brigade shows up. The concerts aren’t just any concerts where no one know the people playing, thay feature Poland’s most famous singers, for example Maryla Rodowicz and Marek Dyjak. So over all, if you want to help, and get some fun in return go to the “GRAND FINALE” from The Great Orchestra of Christmas Charity.
